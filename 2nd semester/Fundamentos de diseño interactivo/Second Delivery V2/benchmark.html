Why Knocki?

An endless maze of mobile apps, smart switches, and other interfaces create a confusing and fragmented experience.
Knocki offers the simplest, most natural and accessible way to control the world around you.

What about competition?

Welle

By Maxus Tech

Welle turns any surface into a smart surface. This device is a finger movement tracker powered by embedded Sonar and instantly control any device anywhere with custom-defined gestures. Supports a variety of gestures, including directional gestures, rotation gestures, alphabetic gestures.

Price: $69
Disadvantages: Strong loud could interfere with the ultrasound and not detect the gestures. This device cannot be mounted and it's easy to lose.

August

By August Home, Inc.

Lock and unlock your door from anywhere, grant secure access to friends and family, and track who is coming and going, all from the August app. Automatically unlocks when you arrive and locks when you leave. If you lose your phone, you can disable your August app and all virtual keys on any associated devices.

Price: $229
Disadvantages: It con only be used to upgrade your doors and make them smart. August don't supports to control another device.

Flic

By Shortcut Labs

Flic is a smart button that allows you to control music, lights and every connected device you have at home. This button can be mounted everywhere. Weather-resistant, outdoor-indoor wireless buttons have up to 18 month battery life.

Price: $35
Disadvantages: Flic is a small device, it needs to be placed in a reachable place to control.
